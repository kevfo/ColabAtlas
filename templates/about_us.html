About this Website

This page provides more information on ColabAtlas and its objectives.

Q: What are our lab's objectives?

A: Mutwil lab is interested in studying the evolution of plant genes and metabolites across the evolution of land plants. We aim to complete the collection of RNA-sequencing data for at least one species from each order listed on the website. The lab is currently collecting plant samples for species without publicly-available RNA sequencing data (denoted in __ on this page).

Q: How do I contribute to the study?

A: If you would like to contribute to our sampling, please explore our species trees and contact us by joining our discord should you have access to plants with missing or limited RNA sequencing data.
